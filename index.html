Chances You May Have COVID-19, Seasonal Allergies, A Cold, Or The Flu

During these troublesome times, it's important to not overburden the healthcare system. However, people also need to understand the chances that they have COVID-19 if they are exhibiting symptoms of respiratory distress. This app aims to help people distinguish between their chances of having COVID-19, as opposed to the chances of having seasonal allergies, a cold, or the flu.
