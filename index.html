Heat Exhaustion or Heat Stroke?

Many people do not understand the difference between these two conditions, as they are both brought on by prolonged exposure to the sun. This confusion could be deadly, as while exhaustion just requires removal and cold remedies, stroke is an extremely serious condition and must receive emergency medical intervention. Fill out the questions below to understand the likelihood you are suffering from heat exhaustion or heat stroke.
